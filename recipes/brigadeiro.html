Brigadeiro

Description

A traditional dessert from Brazil, the favourite of kids and adults. Its very easy to make and you need very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 395g of sweetened condensed milk
  • 30g of cocoa power
  • 160g chocolate sprinkle

Steps

  1. In a pot over low heat, melt the butter, condensed milk, and cocoa powder,
    stirring continuously until you can see the bottom of the pot for 2-3 seconds
    when draggin a spatula through.
  2. Pour onto a greased plate, then chill for 1 hour
  3. Shape and roll the chilled mixture into balls
  4. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles
  5. Enjoy!
