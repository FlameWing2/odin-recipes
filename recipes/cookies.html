Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These cookies are ideal for a meal in the afternoon and very easy to make.
You'll just have to keep an eye on the time so the cookies get crispy only on the outside!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until it smooths
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at the time and then stir in the vanilla
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to batter along with salt
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and nuts
  6. Drop large spoonfuls of the mix onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven until the edges are nicely browned
