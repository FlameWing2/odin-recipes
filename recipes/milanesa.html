Argentinian Milanesas

One of the most typical meals from Argentina. It consists of marinated meat,
covered in bread crumbs and fried in oil on a pan.
Milanesas are perfect for sandwiches and goes well with mashed or fried potatoes, or even a salad!

Ingredients

  • 500 g of meat for milanesas (steaks not very thick should be good enough)
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g Bread crumbs
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoon of Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Remove the fat and nerves that you can from the steak and cut it on slices
  2. Tenderize the meat with a meat hammer
  3. Mince the parsley and put it in a bowl
  4. Peel and mince the garlic cloves and mix them with the parsley
  5. Add salt and pepper to the mix
  6. Put the meat slices on the bowl with the mix
  7. Grab a plate and put some bread crumbs on it
  8. Grab a slice of meat and cover it well on bread crumbs then leave it on a clean plate
  9. Heat up a good amount of oil on a frying pan
  10. Once the oil is hot, fry the meat slices
  11. Take them out when ready (It usually takes around 2 minutes from each side)
  12. Dry them in a plate with some kitchen paper
